Risk: Hallucinations & TrustMitigation: Evidence‑first RAG
All provider claims in Dynamic Matrices and briefs are backed by cited documentation, with an evidence coverage score. Provider facts sit in deterministic tables, and language models are constrained to explain rather than invent them. [file:22][file:38]
Risk: Adoption & Advisor FatigueMitigation: Confidence gating
Coaching signals are confidence‑gated and throttle‑controlled, with a “quiet mode” option. Advisors still receive high‑value automated artifacts (briefs, emails, notes) even with fewer on‑screen prompts. [file:22]
Risk: Data Quality & IntegrationMitigation: Schema‑first + API‑first
Explicit “unknown” flags, verification timestamps, and an API‑first architecture allow ATOM to adopt Pathfinder and Atlas data as‑is, hardening over time without breaking existing workflows. [file:22][file:38]
